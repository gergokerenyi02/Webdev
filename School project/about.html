ABOUT US

TENNESSI Jewelry started unofficially in 2022. That’s when we started to design our first pieces and decided to make high-end jewelry rather than fast-fashion pieces. We knew, that designing custom pieces is very time consuming and it will have some difficulties and it’s own challenges. But we will stay on this road and keep coming with one of the best designs in custom jewelry making.

More about us

Welcome to TENNESSI, your destination for exquisite high-quality silver jewelry with Moissanite stones. Our company specializes in creating unique and beautiful pieces of jewelry that are carefully crafted to meet our customers' desires.

At TENNESSI, we take great pride in using only the highest quality materials to create our stunning jewelry. We believe that every piece of jewelry we create should not only be beautiful but also long-lasting and durable. That's why we use only the finest silver and Moissanite stones in our designs.

Moissanite stones are known for their exceptional brilliance and sparkle, making them an excellent alternative to traditional diamonds. We believe that every person should be able to own a piece of jewelry that makes them feel confident and beautiful, which is why we offer a wide variety of Moissanite jewelry pieces.

Our jewelry is constructed and designed by our team of experts who take great care in creating each piece. We understand that every person has their unique style, which is why we offer a wide variety of designs to choose from. Whether you prefer a classic, timeless piece or a more modern and contemporary design, we have something that will suit your tastes.

For custom design purposes, at TENNESSI we are open to make your dream piece.

At TENNESSI, we believe that every piece of jewelry should be a work of art. That's why we put a great deal of time and effort into each of our designs. From the initial concept to the final product, we work hard to create pieces that our customers will love and cherish for years to come.

Thank you for choosing TENNESSI. We look forward to helping you find the perfect piece of jewelry that will make you feel beautiful, confident, and stylish.
